# M001: Mobile-first Lancotech Website

## Goal

Ship a premium mobile-first Lancotech landing page that communicates learning,
practice, simulation, progress tracking, strategy improvement, and confidence
through guided market education.

## Success Criteria

- Hero communicates "Learn Trading Through Guided Market Simulation."
- Primary CTA is `Start Free`; secondary CTA is `Watch Demo`.
- Hero includes custom dashboard visuals for candlesticks, analytics, portfolio,
  AI assistant, and learning progress.
- Page includes required sections: Hero, Trust Badges, Features, Why Lancotech,
  Dashboard Preview, Learning Journey, Testimonials, Statistics, Pricing, FAQ,
  Final CTA, and Footer.
- Visual design follows the exact Lancotech palette.
- Page is mobile-first and responsive across desktop.
- Framer Motion is used for reveal, hover, floating, parallax, and counter
  animation.
- `npm run build` passes.
