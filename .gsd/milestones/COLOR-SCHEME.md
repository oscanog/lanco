# Lancotech Trading - Color Scheme

## Core Colors
* **Background:** Light Gray (`#F5F5F5`)
* **Primary Text:** Dark Gray (`#424242` and `#262626`)
* **Accents / Gradients:**
  * Teal / Aqua: `#48CFCB`
  * Deep Teal: `#229799`
* **Card Backgrounds:** White with opacity (`bg-white/72`, `bg-white/65`, `bg-[#fbfbfb]`)
* **Borders:** Subtle white or dark transparency (`border-white/80`, `border-[#424242]/8`)

## Typography & Details
* Sans-serif font (Inter / system defaults)
* Shadows for depth on cards `shadow-[0_18px_45px_rgba(66,66,66,0.08)]`
* Gradients used on numbers/icons/buttons: `from-[#48CFCB] to-[#229799]`
